More About NMITD's Placement Cell

The Pre-Placement Preparatory Programme (4Ps ) at NMITD,
is a comprehensive initiative designed to equip students
with the essential skills and knowledge required to
excel in the competitive job market.

This program offers a range of workshops,
seminars, mock interviews, resume building sessions,
and industry interactions that help participants
enhance their employability quotient.
